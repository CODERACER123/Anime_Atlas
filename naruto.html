Story overview

From lonely prankster to leader of the village.

Act 1: The misfit ninja

Naruto Uzumaki grows up shunned by his village, yet he refuses to give up. His rivalry with Sasuke and mentorship under Kakashi form the heart of Team 7.

Act 2: Bonds tested

The Chūnin Exams, the search for Tsunade, and Sasuke’s departure force Naruto to mature while learning the power of trust.

Act 3: War and legacy

Ninja alliances form to stop the Akatsuki and the Fourth Great Ninja War, revealing truths about the past and Naruto’s destiny.

Finale: Hokage dream

Naruto’s unwavering empathy unites friends and foes, fulfilling his promise to protect the village and become Hokage.

Fun facts

Production and lore highlights for Naruto fans.

The series inspired a global ninja boom.

Naruto’s headbands, hand signs, and chakra lore became iconic symbols in pop culture.

The story spans two major series.

Naruto and Naruto Shippuden together cover the character’s growth from academy student to war hero.

“Believe it!” was a signature phrase.

Naruto’s catchphrase ("dattebayo") emphasized his upbeat, never quit attitude.
