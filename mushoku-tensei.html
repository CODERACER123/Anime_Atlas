Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation

A 34-year-old man who wasted his life dies and reincarnates as Rudeus Greyrat — born into a magic-rich fantasy world with all his memories intact. This time, he has decided to actually try.

Core Themes

  • A second chance and the responsibility that comes with it
  • Overcoming psychological trauma through action
  • The long, uneven road of genuine self-improvement

Story overview

A wasted life — and what happens when it gets a second chance.

Arc 1: Infancy and Childhood

Rudeus is born to a noble-adjacent family and immediately begins studying magic with an adult's determination. He forms a bond with his tutor Roxy and childhood friend Sylphiette, developing genuine relationships for the first time in any life.

Arc 2: The Displacement Event

A catastrophic magical event scatters the Greyrat family across the world. Rudeus is teleported to unfamiliar territory with only a young girl, Eris, for company. Their journey home across a changed continent shapes both of them.

Arc 3: Magic University & College Life

Rudeus attends the Ranoa Magic University — a step toward finding his displaced family members. He navigates academic politics, new relationships, and the emotional aftermath of Eris's departure.

Ongoing: The Laplace Factor

The god Hitogami, a mysterious figure seen in dreams, feeds Rudeus information — but his motives are unclear. Against him stands the Human God's enemy, whose agenda slowly shapes the series' larger destiny arc.

Fun facts

Behind the isekai that defined a generation.

Widely credited with revitalising the isekai genre.

Its serious approach to world-building, character psychology, and genuine consequences raised the bar for isekai storytelling significantly when it began publishing in 2012.

The magic system is detailed and internally consistent.

Magic ranks, affinities, spell categories, mana pools, and specialisations are developed with notable depth, making the world feel real.

Studio Bind was created specifically to animate this series.

A new animation studio was founded by White Fox veterans for the sole purpose of adapting Mushoku Tensei — a testament to how highly regarded the source material was.

The three heroines

  • Sylphiette — childhood friend, silent type
  • Eris Boreas Greyrat — fierce, impulsive cousin
  • Roxy Migurdia — first teacher, blue-haired mage
