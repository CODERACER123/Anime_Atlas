Story overview

A chess match between genius minds.

Act 1: The notebook appears

Light Yagami discovers the Death Note and begins using it to punish criminals, gaining the name Kira.

Act 2: L enters the game

The detective L challenges Light, turning the case into a battle of strategy and surveillance.

Act 3: The task force struggle

Alliances shift as more Death Notes appear, complicating the hunt and deepening the mystery.

Finale: The cost of power

Light’s ambition escalates until the truth catches up with him in a final confrontation.
