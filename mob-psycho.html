Fun facts

Behind the ???% of Mob Psycho 100.

Created by ONE — same author as One Punch Man.

Both series subvert the power-fantasy genre with deeply human emotional cores. ONE originally drew Mob Psycho with intentionally rough art.

Studio Bones pushed animation boundaries.

The show is celebrated for its wildly expressive, fluid animation — especially during psychic battles — that has influenced action animation globally.

Reigen is one of anime's best con-men.

Despite having zero psychic ability, Reigen repeatedly outsmarts genuine espers through sheer audacity, making him an audience fan-favorite.
