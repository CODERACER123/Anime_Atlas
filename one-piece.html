Story overview

The Straw Hat Pirates’ journey across a vast world.

Act 1: East Blue beginnings

Luffy forms his first crew, proving that loyalty and courage matter more than fame when facing ruthless pirates.

Act 2: Grand Line discoveries

New islands introduce ancient mysteries, powerful rivals, and escalating stakes with the World Government.

Act 3: A world in upheaval

Alliances shift as Luffy challenges emperors, revealing deeper secrets about the One Piece and the Void Century.

Ongoing: The final voyage

The crew pushes toward the end of the Grand Line, united by their dreams and the promise of freedom.

Fun facts

Milestones and trivia from the high seas.

One Piece is one of the best-selling manga series ever.

Eiichiro Oda’s story holds global records for its longevity and sales.

The world map mirrors real history.

Many islands draw inspiration from historical cultures and myths to build a rich, diverse setting.

The crew’s flags are unique to each member.

Jolly Roger designs and ship emblems capture each pirate’s dream and personality.
