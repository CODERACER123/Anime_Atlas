Story overview

From survival horror to global conflict.

Act 1: The fall of the walls

Eren Yeager joins the Scouts after witnessing tragedy, determined to eradicate the Titans that threaten humanity.

Act 2: Secrets of the Titans

The truth about Titan shifters and the history of the walls changes everything the characters believed.

Act 3: Beyond the sea

The conflict expands to the outside world, revealing political tensions and centuries of prejudice.

Finale: The cost of freedom

The story confronts the price of survival and the weight of inherited hatred.
