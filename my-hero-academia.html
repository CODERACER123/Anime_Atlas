My Hero Academia

In a world where most people have superpowers called quirks, Izuku Midoriya inherits One For All and trains to become the greatest hero.

Story overview

Training arcs, rivalries, and the meaning of heroism.

Act 1: The quirkless dream

Izuku earns All Might’s trust and inherits his power, joining U.A. High to prove that heart matters as much as strength.

Act 2: Trials of U.A.

Training exercises and villain attacks push Class 1-A to develop teamwork and confidence in their abilities.

Act 3: The rise of villains

The League of Villains and the Paranormal Liberation Front raise the stakes, challenging the hero society’s ideals.

Finale: What makes a hero

Izuku learns that heroism is defined by compassion, sacrifice, and inspiring hope in others.
