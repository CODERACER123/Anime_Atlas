Chained Soldier

When Yuuki Wakura is pulled into the demon-infested realm of Mato, he's saved by the commander Kyouka Uzen — but her power comes with a condition: he becomes her slave, bound by a unique contract that lets her reward him as she sees fit.

Story overview

Demons, contracts, and an unlikely soldier.

Act 1: Into Mato

Yuuki wanders into the dangerous realm called Mato, where peaches grant women special powers but ordinary men are helpless. He's rescued by Squad 7 commander Kyouka Uzen after a near-fatal encounter with a demon.

Act 2: The Slave Contract

To fight alongside Squad 7, Yuuki accepts Kyouka's unique contract — becoming her "slave" grants him a massive power boost. Her rewards for good performance take... creative forms.

Act 3: Squad 7 in Action

Yuuki integrates into the squad, each member wielding a unique Peach power. Together they push deeper into Mato to neutralize threats before they spill into the human world.

Escalating Threats

Rival corps and increasingly powerful demons test Squad 7's limits. Yuuki's raw determination and rapidly evolving abilities begin to attract attention from all corners.

Fun facts

Behind the chains and combat of Chained Soldier.

The series subverts the typical power fantasy setup.

Rather than the male lead being the strongest, here women hold dominant power — the series self-consciously plays with those dynamics.

Studio C2C produced season 1.

Known for working on series like Bottom-Tier Character Tomozaki, they brought a slick action-heavy animation style to the series.

The manga continues well beyond the anime.

The source material significantly expands the world of Mato, introducing higher-tier demons and deeper lore about the peach power origins.
