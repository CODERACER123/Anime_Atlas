Story overview

From the weakest hunter to a living legend.

Act 1: The double dungeon

Jinwoo survives a deadly raid and awakens to a system that only he can see, turning his life upside down.

Act 2: The leveling system

He clears quests, gains skills, and starts building a shadow army while keeping his growth a secret.

Act 3: Guilds and rulers

The mystery of the gates deepens as Jinwoo clashes with powerful hunters and supernatural rulers.

Finale: A solo legend

Jinwoo embraces his new identity, fighting to protect humanity from forces beyond the gates.

Fun facts

Highlights from the world of hunters.

Solo Leveling started as a web novel.

It gained massive popularity before becoming a webtoon and anime.

Shadow soldiers are fan favorites.

“Leveling up” is literal.

The system gives RPG-like growth, stats, and quests.
