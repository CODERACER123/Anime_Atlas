Story overview

Volleyball as a journey of growth.

Act 1: Karasuno reborn

Hinata meets Kageyama, and the former rivals become a powerful duo with the “quick attack.”

Act 2: Regional battles

Karasuno faces strong teams like Aoba Johsai and Shiratorizawa while learning to play as one.

Act 3: Nationals challenge

The team competes on the national stage, meeting elite rivals and pushing their limits.

Finale: Beyond the court

Haikyu!! celebrates growth, friendship, and the thrill of the sport itself.
