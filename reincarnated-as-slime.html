That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

Ordinary salaryman Satoru Mikami dies and is reborn in a fantasy world as a slime with the unique skill "Predator" — absorbing enemies to copy their abilities. As Rimuru Tempest, he builds a monster nation based on cooperation and mutual peace.

Story overview

From blob to leader of a nation.

Act 1: A Slime's New World

Rimuru befriends the sealed dragon Veldora, absorbs his power, and begins wandering the forest. He quickly gathers goblins and direwolves under his protection, naming them and granting them new forms.

Act 2: Founding Tempest

Rimuru's village grows into a thriving monster town called Tempest. Dwarven craftsmen, ogre warriors, and lizardmen join his nation as Rimuru's diplomatic philosophy begins to reshape the region.

Act 3: Threats from the Kingdom

Human kingdoms and religious forces view Tempest with suspicion. Rimuru must balance internal development with external threats — not all of which can be solved peacefully.

Act 4: Demon Lord Awakening

A devastating attack on Tempest forces Rimuru to evolve beyond his limits and claim the title of Demon Lord — changing his nature and his nation's place in the world forever.

Fun facts

Behind Rimuru's world of slimes and monsters.

One of the best-selling isekai light novels ever.

The source light novel has sold over 40 million copies worldwide, making it one of the most successful isekai properties in publishing history.

The "naming" mechanic is thematically important.

Rimuru giving names to monsters grants them power and identity — a literal act of care and recognition that defines his leadership philosophy.

Rimuru presents as gender-neutral.

As a slime, Rimuru has no inherent gender, and the series reflects this thoughtfully — he ultimately takes on a form inspired by his previous teacher.
