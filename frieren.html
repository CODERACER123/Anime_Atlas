Story overview

After the hero’s party disbands, a new journey begins.

Act 1: A reunion and a promise

Frieren’s immortal perspective clashes with the fleeting lives of her companions, sparking a vow to understand humans better.

Act 2: The next generation

She mentors Fern and Stark, balancing quiet reflection with powerful magic and growing bonds.

Act 3: Echoes of the past

Old journeys resurface through new quests, revealing hidden feelings and untold memories.

Finale: Meaning in the moments

Frieren learns the true value of the time shared with others—one gentle day at a time.
