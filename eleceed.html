Speed, heart, and a very grumpy cat

Eleceed balances pure comic energy with legitimately hype action sequences.

Jiwoo Seo

Jiwoo's power is deceptively simple ‚Äî speed and reflexes beyond human limit. The manhwa excels at showing how raw speed, used intelligently, scales into something terrifying. His personality is relentlessly good-natured, which makes him easy to root for without being boring.

Kayden Break

Ranked among the world's strongest awakeners, Kayden is now stuck in a chubby cat's body due to an accident. He mentors Jiwoo while desperately trying to return to his human form ‚Äî a dynamic that creates most of the series' best comedy and its most touching moments.

The Awakener World

People with supernatural abilities ‚Äî awakeners ‚Äî operate in a shadow society with factions, rankings, and escalating conflicts. Eleceed builds this world steadily, expanding from street-level fights into global-scale stakes over time.

Why It Works

Eleceed is drawn by Zhena (also the artist on Noblesse) and written by Son Jeho. The art's fluidity makes action scenes feel kinetic on the page, while the character writing keeps even side characters memorable. A rare action manhwa with genuine heart.
