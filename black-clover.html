Story overview

Magic knights and a boy who refuses to quit.

Act 1: The grimoire moment

Asta receives a rare five-leaf grimoire that grants anti-magic, allowing him to fight even without mana.

Act 2: Black Bulls rising

He joins the Black Bulls squad, building confidence while facing dungeon threats and rival squads.

Act 3: Kingdom crises

The Clover Kingdom confronts powerful enemies like the Eye of the Midnight Sun and the Spade Kingdom.

Finale: Asta’s resolve

Asta pushes beyond limits, proving that heart and teamwork can surpass destiny.
