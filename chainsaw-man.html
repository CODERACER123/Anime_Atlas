Story overview

Devil hunting with a heart of chaos.

Act 1: A deal with Pochita

Denji is pushed to the edge and fuses with his devil companion, gaining chainsaw powers and a second chance.

Act 2: Public Safety hunters

Recruited by Makima, Denji joins a devil-hunting squad and learns brutal lessons about trust.

Act 3: Devils and betrayals

Missions against powerful devils reveal deeper conspiracies and test Denji’s loyalty.

Finale: Who controls the chainsaw

Denji fights to define his own identity, even as forces try to weaponize him.
