Story overview

A mission that turns into a heartfelt family story.

Act 1: Operation Strix

Twilight adopts Anya and marries Yor to create a cover family for his mission, each hiding their real identity.

Act 2: Eden Academy

Anya attends an elite school, leading to comedic struggles and unexpected friendships.

Act 3: Double lives collide

Spy missions, assassin contracts, and Anya’s telepathy drive the family closer while chaos unfolds.

Finale: A real family bond

The Forgers grow to care for each other, blurring the line between mission and genuine connection.
