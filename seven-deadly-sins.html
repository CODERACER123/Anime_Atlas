Seven Deadly Sins

Princess Elizabeth searches for the legendary Seven Deadly Sins — a disbanded group of holy knights — to reclaim her kingdom from a corrupt order that has seized control.

Story overview

Legendary knights, ancient evils, and a kingdom to save.

Act 1: Gathering the Sins

Princess Elizabeth finds Meliodas — the Dragon's Sin of Wrath — running a traveling tavern. Together they seek out the other six Sins: Diane, Ban, King, Gowther, Merlin, and Escanor.

Act 2: Holy War Conspiracy

The true threat is revealed: the Ten Commandments, demonic warriors sealed away for thousands of years, are breaking free. Their power dwarfs anything the Sins have faced before.

Act 3: Meliodas's Secret

Meliodas's past as the Demon King's son is uncovered, along with his immortal curse and his connection to Elizabeth's own cursed reincarnation cycle spanning centuries.

Finale: Defeating the Demon King

The Sins unite to face the Demon King himself in a climactic battle that tests every bond formed throughout the series.

Fun facts

Behind the sins and swords.

Each Sin represents a Biblical deadly sin.

Meliodas = Wrath, Diane = Envy, Ban = Greed, King = Sloth, Gowther = Lust, Merlin = Gluttony, Escanor = Pride.

Escanor is one of anime's most beloved characters.

The Pride Sin who transforms from timid to godlike at noon became a fan phenomenon, spawning countless anime-best-character debates.

The Britannia setting draws from Arthurian legend.

Merlin, King Arthur, and the Holy War tie into the mythology of Camelot woven into this fantasy world.

The Seven Deadly Sins

  • Meliodas — Wrath (Dragon)
  • Ban — Greed (Fox)
  • Diane — Envy (Serpent)
  • King — Sloth (Grizzly)
  • Gowther — Lust (Goat)
  • Merlin — Gluttony (Boar)
  • Escanor — Pride (Lion)
