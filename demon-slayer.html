Story overview

Demons, swordsmanship, and unwavering empathy.

Act 1: Tragedy and resolve

Tanjiro’s family is attacked, and his sister Nezuko becomes a demon, propelling him to join the Demon Slayer Corps.

Act 2: Training and allies

He learns breathing techniques, meets Zenitsu and Inosuke, and battles demons across haunting landscapes.

Act 3: The Hashira missions

Elite swordsmen guide Tanjiro as he takes on powerful Upper Rank demons.

Finale: Breaking the curse

The Corps confronts Muzan Kibutsuji, fighting to end the demon threat and restore Nezuko’s humanity.
